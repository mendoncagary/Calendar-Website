Introduction


You observe, question and respond - engaging the world around you through a visual and cultural context. You explore meaning, influence and theory.

The Department of Art and Design at The University of Mumbai in India is training artists, scholars and educators just like you to advance their technique and refine their ideas in the rigorous and exciting setting of a Tier One research university. 70+ exhibitions and lectures each year connect our students in an active and engaging community. Our award-winning faculty and visiting artists are showcased around the world, opening doors to an international network of study abroad, research and internship opportunities. We do all this in a faculty-to-student ratio of 1:10, at a fraction of the cost of our competitors.
